Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products cruelty-free?
Yes, all our cosmetics are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. We are proud to be certified by Leaping Bunny and PETA.
How long do your products last after opening?
Most products have a shelf life of 12-24 months after opening. Look for the period-after-open symbol (6M, 12M, 24M) on each product's packaging.
Can I return products if they don't suit me?
Absolutely! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.
Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?
Many of our products are formulated for sensitive skin. Look for the "Sensitive Skin Friendly" badge on product pages. We recommend doing a patch test first.
Do you offer vegan products?
Over 80% of our collection is vegan, and we're working to make it 100%. Vegan products are clearly marked with a leaf icon on their labels.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can monitor your package's journey directly through our order tracking portal.
